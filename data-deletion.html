Data Deletion Request

To delete your entire account and all associated data on Velohaus.net, please follow the steps below.

Step 1: Send an email to support@velohaus.net

Step 2: Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"

Step 3: Send it from the email address you registered with on Velohaus.net

We will verify and permanently delete your data within 7 business days, then send a confirmation email.
